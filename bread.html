Bread Recipe

Learn how to make a simple and delicious bread from scratch!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, yeast, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, then mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until smooth.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375°F 190°C
  6. Punch down the dough and shape it into a loaf.
  7. Place the loaf in a greased bread pan and let it rise for another 30 minutes.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped.
  9. Let it cool on a wire rack before slicing.
