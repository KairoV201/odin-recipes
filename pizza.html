Pizza Recipe

Learn how to make a delicious pizza from scratch!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add olive oil and warm water, then knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise for about 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  6. Spread pizza sauce over the dough.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
